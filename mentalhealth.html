Mental Health is really important and should be taken care of as it impacts the quality of life. Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being and it affects how we think, feel, and act. There are several ways we can improve our mental health which are by having a proper nutrition, playing games, spending time with friends, joining programs that support mental health and etc. Together we can make the world a better place!
